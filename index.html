About Us

Daarain Foundation is a nonprofit animal welfare organization that rescues and rehabilitates abandoned animals in India. We provide emergency treatment, shelter, and a second chance for countless dogs, cats, and birds. Our mission is to inspire compassion, promote responsible pet care, and create a community where every life matters.
