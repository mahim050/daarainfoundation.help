About Us

Daarain Foundation is a community-powered animal welfare organization dedicated to rescuing injured and abandoned animals, providing urgent medical treatment, compassionate rehabilitation, and responsible adoptions. We partner with local vets, volunteers, and civic groups to build a kinder city for animals and people. Transparency, humane care, and lifelong support are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To rescue, heal, and rehome animals in need while promoting community education and responsible guardianship.

Our Approach

  • 24×7 rescue helpline & intake
  • Clinical care & vaccination
  • Foster network & rehabilitation
  • Careful matching & post‑adoption follow‑ups

Accountability

  • Transparent reporting
  • Ethical treatment & no-kill policy*
  • Community partnerships

*Euthanasia only in cases of untreatable suffering, as advised by licensed vets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about adopting, volunteering, and supporting our work.

How do I adopt?▾

Visit our adoption section, meet animals during visiting hours or camps, and complete a short application. We conduct a home check and offer a trial week to ensure a good match.

Can I volunteer without prior experience?▾

Absolutely! We provide orientation and pair you with experienced volunteers for hands‑on learning.

Do you provide vaccinations and spay/neuter?▾

Yes. All animals receive age‑appropriate vaccinations and are spayed/neutered before adoption (or scheduled promptly if medically delayed).

How are donations used?▾

More than 90% funds go directly to rescue operations, medical care, food, and shelter; the remainder supports logistics and outreach.
